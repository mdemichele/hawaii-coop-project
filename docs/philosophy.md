## Why Cooperatives?

I want to answer the question of how do we give people in Hawaii ownership? How do we incentive individuals to care more about their local community? How do we do so in a way that is not simply another implementation of individualistic capitalism? Western capitalism, with its emphasis on private ownership, is what destroyed Hawaii. How do we build new forms of ownership that rejects this paradigm? That turns us back to Indigenous wisdom. To the wisdom of the Kanaka Maoli. How do we see through new - or rather, very old - eyes? How do we respect place? How do we build shared responsibility into our thinking about ownership. Why can't we try other methods beyond corporate domination?
